What Rite does

Not a trend aggregator. Not another wellness app. UpKeep is a quiet, precise tool — built around you, refined over time — for men who understand that how you look reflects how you live.

  • 01 Skincare UpKeep builds a daily skincare protocol around your skin type, climate, and concerns — adjusting as your skin changes. No guesswork. No wasted shelf space.
  • 02 Facial Hair Shape, length, maintenance — your beard or clean shave managed with precision. UpKeep suggests products, timing, and technique based on your face structure and hair type.
  • 03 Hair Care From scalp health to styling, UpKeep provides a structured, expert-guided hair routine built for how you actually live — not how someone else styles their life.
  • 04 Fragrance Build a considered fragrance wardrobe. Rite helps you understand your scent profile, match fragrances to context, and layer them with intention.
  • 05 Morning Routine Everything, sequenced. UpKeep assembles a precise, time-aware morning routine — the right steps, the right products, the right order — every single morning.
